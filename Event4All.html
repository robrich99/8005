About The Event

The Event4All is an Annual Technology Conference which is open for everyone in the world. The event covers technology related topics which are relevant to almost all industries, such as Finance, Telecommunications, Transport, and Construction.

F.A.Q

  • Who should attend this event?

    This event is open to everyone regardless of the industry you are coming from.

  • What is the duration of the event?

    The event's duration is between 09:00-17:00.

  • Do I need to bring anything with me?

    No.

  • Can I come in late?

    Yes, however we encourage to come in order to benefit from all talks.

  • Can I get a refund?

    Unfortunately, your ticket is not transferable nor refundable.

    Yes, you can find plenty of food and drinks within the venue.
